About Lainoa Software

Hi! My name is Josu and I'm the creator of the simulator.

In 2025, I realised that there was a huge gap in the world of flight simulation. There was no ATC simulator for amateur users that was reliable in terms of voice recognition and focused on approach control. So I decided to take the challenge on my own and build a game from scratch. On april 2025 I released the Early Access version of the game. There is very good feedback from the avgeek community so I look forward to push updates frequently!
